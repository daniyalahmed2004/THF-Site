ABOUT THE HUNAR FOUNDATION THF

Rigistered in 2008 under the Societies Act 1860, THF took its first batch in january 2010 within the premises provide by Delhi Marcantile Society, Karachi in its DMS School facility. The students gave their at the end of the year and till 2021, 15 batches have graduated in 6 month to 1 year diploma courses.
THF has added its cumpuses in:

  • Rashidabad,Tando Allahyar
  • Bedian Road,Lahore
  • Pak Arab Fertilizer,Multan
  • GT Road,Kharian
  • Sadder,Karachi

to Emerging Technology Program,Call Center Traning and various other courses.

VISION

creating a new cadre of skilled pakistanis with internationally recognized technical qualificatin, opening up a new world of opportunities. i.e A SKILLED PAKISTAN

MISSION

To initiate a movement to skill the motivated illiterate ,semi-literate and literate: To provide technicval training 'with a difference' in quality of skill as well as ethical, social and moral values.

At present,THF has eight operational institutes across Pakistan which produced 8000 skilled graduates (Boys & Girls) since 2010.With more institutes to become operational in upcoming months,THF plans to increase the number of purpose built Vocational & Technical intitutes.
